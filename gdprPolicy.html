This Privacy Policy outlines how Prieli.com ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We may collect personal data such as name, email address, and contact details when you interact with our website, subscribe to our newsletters, or engage with our content.

  • Usage Data: We gather non-personal data about your interactions with our website, including IP addresses, browser information, and pages visited. This data is used for analytics and website improvement purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • We collect and process personal data for the purpose of delivering content, providing newsletters, and improving user experience.

  • Usage data is used to analyze website performance, optimize content, and enhance user engagement.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • We process personal data based on your consent, which is obtained when you subscribe to newsletters or engage with interactive elements on our website.

  • We may also process data based on legitimate interests, including analytics and website improvement.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. However, we may share data with service providers who assist in website operations, analytics, and marketing.

  • We may share aggregated and anonymized data with advertisers for the purpose of delivering targeted advertisements.

Data Retention

  • Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

  • You can unsubscribe from our newsletters or update your preferences using the provided links.

Data Security

  • We implement security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

International Data Transfers

  • Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your jurisdiction. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our GDPR policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at contact@prieli.com.
